System Overview

The evaluation system is designed to provide comprehensive feedback across different roles and academic phases. It includes:

  • Four academic phases: Early, Mid, Post-Mid, and Final Semester
  • Multiple roles: HOD, Teacher, Student, Peer, Principal, and Parent
  • Detailed criteria specific to each role and phase
  • A 1-4 rating scale for consistent evaluation

Rating Scale

All evaluations use the following 1-4 scale:

1 Needs Improvement
2 Developing
3 Proficient
4 Exemplary

Use this scale to provide accurate and fair assessments.

How to Complete an Evaluation

Follow these steps to complete an evaluation:

  1. Select Academic Phase: Choose the appropriate semester phase from the dropdown.
  2. Select Role: Pick the role you are evaluating (options vary by phase).
  3. Fill Required Fields: Enter all mandatory information (marked with *).
  4. Rate Criteria: Provide ratings for each criteria using the 1-4 scale.
  5. Review: Check your entries for accuracy.
  6. Submit: Click the submit button to save your evaluation.

Note: All required fields must be completed. The system will automatically calculate average scores for each section.

Role-Specific Guidelines

Each role has unique evaluation criteria:

  • HOD: Focuses on leadership, record-keeping, and professional conduct
  • Teacher: Assesses teaching methods, relationships, and professional growth
  • Student: Split into K-2 and III-XII with age-appropriate questions
  • Peer: Evaluates collaboration and teamwork
  • Principal: Reviews administrative and teaching responsibilities
  • Parent: Gauges classroom environment and communication

Tips for Effective Evaluation

  • Be objective and honest in your ratings
  • Provide ratings based on observed behavior or evidence
  • Take your time to ensure accuracy
  • Contact support if you encounter any issues
